Leetcode: Best SightSeeing Pair

Problem: Maximize values[i] + values[j] + i - j.

Approach - Brute Force

Let us group factors related to i together and similarly factors related to j together. This is like "Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock" where instead of finding the max(price[j] - price[i]), we're looking for max((values[i] + i) + (values[j] - j))
